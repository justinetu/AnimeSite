  • Clannad and Clannad After Story


Clannad.com
This by far is my favorite anime as it is like no other anime I have ever seen before. We get to see the characters grow from
when they are in highschool till they are grown adults not only that but this anime takes viewers on an emotional roller coaster
with its many tragic scenes leaving viewers happy, sad, and scared at times. All in all, I give this anime a 10 out of 10.

  • Full Metal Alchemist Brotherhood


fmab.com
This anime is arguably the best anime of all time to many. This is one of my favorites as it's packed with action and has a near perfect story line.

  • Sword Art Online


sao.com
This anime I thought was rather unique the whole idea of being mentally trapped in a virtual reality game against your will and the only possible way of escaping being to defeat the game. This anime for me was emotional at times but I enjoyed it's action packed scenes. Overall, I give this one a 7 out of 10
